The Hadamard gate and a variant of the Hadamard gate with a measure at the end

\begin{align}
    H' = \begin{bmatrix}
        \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} & -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \\
        \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} & \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \\
        \end{bmatrix}  
\end{align}

Both the Hadamard gate and the $H'$ gates will produce same distribution on any basic states,
but as long as it is a pure state, the interference of the Hadamard gate will produce different result.
