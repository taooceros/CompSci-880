$$
\rho=\frac{1}{2}\begin{pmatrix}

$$
H \rho H^* = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix} 
    1 & 1\\
    1 & -1 
\end{pmatrix} \frac{1}{2}\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & -i\\
    i & 1

    1 & 1\\
    1 & -1 
\end{pmatrix} 
= \frac{1}{4}\begin{pmatrix} 
    2 & 2i\\
    -2i & 2 
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Therefore it doesn't do anything, so the conclusion makes sense.
